Part 1: The Orange Pet CatDeep in a dark forest, a group of wild cats called ThunderClan is in trouble. Their leader is a cat named Bluestar. She is a large, silver-gray cat with clear blue eyes and a scar across her shoulders. Bluestar talks to a medicine cat named Spottedleaf. Spottedleaf is a beautiful, small tortoiseshell cat. Spottedleaf looks at the sky and receives a magical message from the stars: a special fire will rescue their cat family.  Not far from the forest, a young pet cat named Rusty is sitting in his garden. Rusty is a handsome cat with bright orange, ginger fur. He lives in a safe human house, but he always dreams of hunting in the wild woods. One night, his friend Smudge comes to see him. Smudge is a plump black-and-white pet cat with big amber eyes. Smudge warns Rusty that the forest is full of scary, wild cats, but Rusty is too curious to stay home.  Rusty jumps over his fence and goes into the woods to catch a mouse. Suddenly, he is attacked by a wild cat! Rusty is brave and fights back, pinning the attacker down. He finds out his attacker is actually a friendly young wild cat named Graypaw. Graypaw is a kitten cat with fluffy gray fur, a wide face, and bright yellow eyes.  While the two young cats are talking, two adult wild cats step out of the shadows. One is Bluestar, the silver-gray leader cat. The other is Lionheart, who is a huge, magnificent golden tabby cat with bright green eyes. They are very surprised that a soft pet cat like Rusty is so brave and knows how to fight so well!
