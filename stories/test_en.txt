A lone gunslinger named Jax rides into a lawless desert town at high noon.
The streets are empty. Behind a saloon window, an outlaw boss named Cronk watches him, grinning.
Jax dismounts, his hand hovering over his revolver. "Cronk! Come out and face me!"
Cronk kicks open the saloon doors and steps into the sun, six henchmen fanning out behind him.
"You've got guts, kid. Too bad this is where you die." Jax's eyes narrow. In a flash, both draw their guns.
